## 🖥️ Manage Desktop Icons

### 🔹 Step 1: Show/Hide Desktop Icons

1. Right-click on Desktop
2. Click **View**
3. Select **Show desktop icons**

<img width="1307" height="807" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d4ff7ead-0db5-4dc7-a333-0b10d9a5ac84" />


---

### 🔹 Step 2: Add System Icons (This PC, Recycle Bin)

1. Press **Windows + I**
2. Go to **Personalization → Themes**
3. Click **Desktop icon settings**
4. Select:

   * This PC
   * Recycle Bin
   * Network

<img width="1919" height="1079" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/61476e6a-3865-401d-b98e-2538345c1a81" />


---

### 🔹 Step 3: Arrange Icons

* Right-click Desktop → **View**

  * Auto arrange icons
  * Align to grid

---

### 🔹 Step 4: Change Icon Size

* Right-click Desktop → View → Select:

  * Large icons
  * Medium icons
  * Small icons

---

## 🎨 Change System Theme & Wallpaper

### 🔹 Step 1: Open Personalization

1. Press **Windows + I**
2. Go to **Personalization**

---

### 🔹 Step 2: Change Theme

1. Click **Themes**
2. Choose any theme:

   * Light
   * Dark
   * Custom

### 🔹 Step 3: Change Wallpaper

1. Go to **Background**

2. Select:

   * Picture
   * Solid color
   * Slideshow

3. Click **Browse** → Select image

## 🌍 Configure Date, Time, and Region

### 🔹 Step 1: Open Settings

1. Press **Windows + I**
2. Go to **Time & Language**

---

### 🔹 Step 2: Set Date & Time

1. Click **Date & Time**
2. Enable:

   * Set time automatically
   * Set time zone automatically
