# 🖥️ Customize Start Menu & Taskbar in Windows 11

This guide explains how to customize the **Start Menu and Taskbar in Windows 11** with step-by-step instructions and screenshots.

---

# 🎯 1. Customize Start Menu

## 🔹 Step 1: Open Start Menu

* Press **Windows Key** or click the Start icon
  
---

## 🔹 Step 2: Pin Apps to Start

1. Search for any app
2. Right-click → **Pin to Start**

![Pin App](images/pin-app.png)

---

## 🔹 Step 3: Unpin Apps

1. Right-click on pinned app
2. Click **Unpin from Start**

---

## 🔹 Step 4: Rearrange Apps

* Drag and drop apps to reorder

![Rearrange Apps](images/rearrange.png)

---

## 🔹 Step 5: Change Start Layout

1. Press **Windows + I**
2. Go to **Personalization → Start**

![Start Settings](images/start-settings.png)

### Options:

* More pins
* More recommendations
* Show recently added apps

---

# 🎯 2. Customize Taskbar

## 🔹 Step 6: Open Taskbar Settings

* Right-click on Taskbar → **Taskbar settings**

![Taskbar Settings](images/taskbar-settings.png)

---

## 🔹 Step 7: Change Taskbar Alignment

* Go to **Taskbar behaviors**

![Taskbar Alignment](images/taskbar-alignment.png)

### Choose:

* Center (default)
* Left (Windows 10 style)

---

## 🔹 Step 8: Show/Hide Icons

Toggle ON/OFF:

* Search
* Widgets
* Task View

![Taskbar Icons](images/taskbar-icons.png)

---

## 🔹 Step 9: Pin Apps to Taskbar

1. Open app
2. Right-click → **Pin to taskbar**

---

## 🔹 Step 10: Unpin Apps

* Right-click → **Unpin from taskbar**

---

## 🔹 Step 11: Auto Hide Taskbar

1. Go to **Taskbar behaviors**
2. Enable **Automatically hide the taskbar**

---

# 🔥 Bonus Tips

* 🎨 Change Colors → Settings → Personalization → Colors
* 🧩 Use Widgets (Windows + W)
* 🖥️ Use Virtual Desktops (Windows + Tab)
